# Documentation Migration and Information Architecture Projects

## Overview

Led and contributed to multiple large-scale documentation migration and restructuring initiatives across enterprise and product documentation environments, focused on improving information architecture, scalability, searchability, and long-term governance.

These projects involved migrating legacy documentation systems, redesigning organizational structures, standardizing content frameworks, and optimizing documentation workflows across multiple platforms and departments.

## Core Responsibilities

My responsibilities across these initiatives included conducting documentation audits and inventories, designing information architecture structures, defining categorization and labeling systems, creating content standards and templates, coordinating migration workflows, validating content organization and compliance, and collaborating with stakeholders across technical, support, and product teams.

## Selected Projects

### Atlassian – SOP Centralization Initiative

Led a strategic initiative to centralize and reorganize Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) from six separate support teams into Atlassian’s CSS SOP Space within Confluence.

The project involved auditing dispersed documentation, conducting collaborative categorization exercises with stakeholders, defining standardized labeling and taxonomy systems, validating compliance requirements, and enforcing content governance standards to improve discoverability and long-term maintainability.

Due to confidentiality restrictions, supporting materials for this project cannot be publicly shared.

### Zenvia – Help Center Migration

Contributed to the migration of Zenvia’s legacy Help Center into a new Movidesk Knowledge Base platform.

Responsibilities included redefining content standards, designing updated information architecture, creating scalable organizational structures, migrating legacy content, and maintaining parallel production of new manuals, guides, and technical specifications throughout the transition process.

Supporting Material:
https://zenvia.movidesk.com/kb

### Blockbit – Resource Center Migration

Participated in the migration and restructuring of Blockbit’s Resource Center into Atlassian Confluence.

This included manually adapting legacy documentation due to format compatibility limitations, implementing templates and structural standards, improving navigation and usability, supporting multilingual documentation expansion, and maintaining continuous updates aligned with product releases.

Supporting Material:
https://docs.blockbit.com/

## Key Challenges

These initiatives required balancing large-scale structural reorganization with ongoing documentation maintenance responsibilities, while ensuring content consistency, technical accuracy, and minimal disruption to operational teams during migration periods.

Additional complexity stemmed from working across multiple platforms, handling incompatible legacy formatting, and designing structures capable of supporting future scalability.

## Outcome

The successful completion of these initiatives resulted in improved documentation accessibility, stronger content governance, more scalable maintenance workflows, enhanced searchability, and better alignment between documentation systems and operational team needs.

Collectively, these projects strengthened internal documentation operations and improved knowledge accessibility for technical teams, support staff, and end users.
