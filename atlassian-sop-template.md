# Atlassian SOP Template Development

## Overview

Contributed to the development of the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) template currently available in Atlassian’s official Confluence template library for external users.

## Responsibilities

My responsibilities included contributing to the design of the template’s structural framework, defining information hierarchy, shaping tone and language standards, and helping establish visual/document flow elements to ensure clarity, consistency, and usability. I also contributed to aligning the template structure with internal documentation best practices for operational use.

## Challenges

One of the primary challenges of this project was balancing professional procedural standards with accessibility, ensuring the template remained structured enough for enterprise use while still being intuitive and approachable for a wide range of users.

## Outcome

The final template was officially adopted into Atlassian’s public Confluence template library, providing customers and partners with a practical framework for creating reusable and clearly structured operational procedures.
