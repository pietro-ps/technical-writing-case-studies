# Blockbit Appliance Manual Production

## Overview

Led the creation and maintenance of appliance manuals for Blockbit’s hardware product line, covering installation, maintenance, operational, and safety procedures for physical security appliances distributed across domestic and international markets.

This project expanded beyond software documentation and required producing technical content tailored for hardware deployment and handling.

## Responsibilities

My responsibilities included gathering technical requirements from engineering and support teams, writing and structuring instructional content, developing a standardized manual template for future scalability, producing and editing supporting visual assets, coordinating bilingual publication in Portuguese and English, and maintaining documentation organization through Confluence.

## Challenges

The project required balancing technical depth with accessibility, ensuring the manuals were detailed enough for technical personnel while remaining understandable for end users and deployment teams.

Additional complexity came from the need to align the materials with technical and regulatory standards while preparing them for use across multiple international markets.

## Documentation Strategy

The manuals were developed using a collaborative and iterative workflow.

Initial technical requirements were collected from engineering and support teams, after which draft materials were created and submitted for technical validation. Feedback cycles were used to refine content and ensure technical accuracy.

To improve consistency and scalability, a standardized template structure was developed to streamline future appliance manual creation and updates.

Supporting visual materials were produced internally through original photography and image editing to provide clear step-by-step installation and maintenance references.

## Outcome

The final deliverable consisted of a complete appliance manual suite covering multiple Blockbit hardware models, published in both Portuguese and English.

The manuals became a core resource for customer deployment, technical support, internal training, and sales enablement, providing standardized and accessible hardware documentation across the organization.
